
Database will consist of multiple tables.

#1 "Planets" :

- ID (INT NOT NULL UNIQUE PRIMARY KEY)
- Name (VARCHAR20)
- Type by composition (varchar20)
- Number of Moons (int)
- Star (int references table stars)
- Mass (10^24 kg) (float)
- Diameter (km) (float)
- Density (kg/m^3) (float)
- Gravity (m/s^2) (float)
- Length of day Rnd. (hours) (int)
- Mean Temperature (Float)
- Habitability (Boolean)
